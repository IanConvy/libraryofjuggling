Infinity

Other Tutorials:

  • Siteswap: (4,4)(4x,0)*
  • Difficulty (1-10): 3
  • Prerequisites: Columns
  • Related Tricks: Overthrow Variation, Reverse Infinity

Bart Griffith (demonstration)

     The Infinity is a variation of Columns in which the center ball, rather than just traveling up and down, is thrown through the two outside balls in a sideways figure-eight pattern (hence the name). Like many of the simple Columns variations, this trick is fairly self-explanatory, and does not require a major tutorial. Just start with a normal Columns pattern, and add in a Cascade-like throw between the outside balls to your other hand. Repeat this on both sides and you have the Infinity.
