Columns: Overthrow Variation

     The Overthrow variation of Columns is a trick in which the center ball, normally thrown vertically, is instead thrown back and forth over the two outside balls. This is a fairly easy pattern to learn, therefore an in-depth tutorial is not really necessary. Just start out juggling the normal Columns, and then make an overthrow with the inside ball. It may take a couple of tries for you to figure out how strongly to throw the ball with each hand, but after that you should have no problems.
