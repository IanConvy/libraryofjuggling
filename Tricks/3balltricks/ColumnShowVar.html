     The Shower variation of the Columns pattern is a little trick I came up while trying out variations on the standard pattern—though the trick itself has been performed by others before me. Basically you make continuous vertical throws with two balls while passing a third over and underneath, as if juggling the Shower. There really isn't much material to use for a tutorial—once you have both the Columns and Shower patterns down you should have little difficulty figuring out this trick. Just keep in mind that the Shower portion of the trick will feel rushed, and it may take some time for you to get comfortable with the speed.
